Bio

KR

"Music is the language I speak when words aren't enough."

Keith RSA is a rising force in the South African hip-hop scene, delivering bars forged from lived experience, street wisdom, and relentless ambition. Born and raised in South Africa, his sound bridges the raw energy of local rap culture with a global sonic vision.

His music speaks to the grind — to those who hustle in the shadows and dream in the light. With every track, Keith RSA carves deeper into the culture, building a legacy one verse at a time.
